ROMU


Wyss Institute, 2018

Terramanus Ferromurus (Romu) is an autonomous miniature sheet pile driving robot capable of building check dams that could be used for soil stabilization and groundwater recharge. Romu is intended to be deployed as part of a decentralized construction collective, using environmental cues to guide building activity.

HONG KONG UNIVERSITY

Shanghai, China, 2012-2014

A primary component of teaching Visual Communications II (Material Culture) was providing instruction for 3D modeling and rendering using Rhino and Maxwell, respectively. Students were challenged to 3D model a series of architectural elements, then combine them into a shared repository. Finally, students were required to design an assembly from the shared elements, and render the result. Featured work by students Yang Qi, Wu Hou Lam, Huang Danlei, Wing Fok, Shao Yue, Hyunah Kim, Anthony Ka Lee

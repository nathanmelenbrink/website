NuVu STUDIO


Between 2015-2017, I taught a number of 2-3 week studios at NuVu Studio, an innovation school for grades 6-12 in Cambridge, MA. A recurring theme of these studios was distributed/swarm robotics for construction. In addition to teaching in Cambridge, the images here show the results of a visiting studio in Mumbai, India where students used the Ringo robot, as well as another studio at Kelvinside Academy in Glasgow, Scotland, which used a custom robot.

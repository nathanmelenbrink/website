How to Design (almost) Anything

Cambridge, 2017

Worked collaboratively to develop and teach a new course at MIT's School of Architecture and Planning called How to Design (almost) Anything, modeled as a sequel to How to Make (almost) Anything and providing an introductory design-studio based course for non-designers. Contributions included original curriculum development, lecture content, studio critiques and evaluations as well as the design and development of core course materials. Featured student work: Kevin Jiang, Mina Khan, Sneha Makini
